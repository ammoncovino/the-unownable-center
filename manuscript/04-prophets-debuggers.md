# Prophets as Non-Coercive Debuggers

Recovered structural placement:

The prophets are read as system debuggers with no coercive power.

Core claims to develop:

- Prophets name drift: idolatry, injustice, exploitation, ritual substitution, and false peace.
- They often lack direct control over temple, king, army, market, or law.
- Their function is exposure, not administration.
- They diagnose structural misalignment before collapse makes the diagnosis obvious.

Bridge:

If prophets are debuggers, then the red sayings can be read as an interface: not just content, but a way of training perception so drift can be recognized before exposure becomes catastrophic.
